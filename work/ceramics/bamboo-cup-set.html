Bamboo Cup Set

2024. Stoneware with glaze. 12×12×14cm.
This is simply a practice series to experiment with the idea of stacking up dinnerwares. Such products are probably already available online from worldwide manufacturers. The experimentation to match the diameters of each cup is similar to the process of drawing from life: to make it as accurate as possible.
