Burger Dish Set

2022. Stoneware with glaze. 20cm×20cm×25cm.
Inspired by the piled bowls in cabinet, a hamburger outline is employed to give the dish set more fun. The curve of the bowls is replaced by a trapezoidal shape to enhance the stability when stacked up. Explore various ways to make your favorite burger with the same set of ingredients.
