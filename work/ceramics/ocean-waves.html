Ocean Waves

2023. Stoneware with glaze. 90×15×45 cm.
The  ocean waves somehow imply the social relationships between people, their interaction, in a macroscopic view: the influence you exert on others may not turn out exactly the same way you expect, but it will always result in some influence.
The process of making their curves match was all about eyeballing. It is always satisfying to see them match, as if two puzzle pieces finally find each other. The purpose was to make them as individually functional vases, but when they are together, a greater harmony is shown.
